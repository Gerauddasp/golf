Situations

  • Challenge: No margin for error - must catch ball first
  • Club Choice: Hybrid or iron preferred over woods
  • Ball Position: Center or slightly back
  • Weight: 60% on front foot
  • Swing: Descending blow, no scooping
  • Contact: Ball first, then turf (crisp impact)
  • Warning: Don't try to help ball up - trust loft
