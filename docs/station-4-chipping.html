Practice Exercises

Circle Drill
SetupPlace tees in 1m circle around hole
Reps10 chips from 5m, 10 from 10m
Goal8/10 inside the circle
Success: 8/10 chips finish inside the 1m circle
Up and Down Challenge
SetupChip from different spots, putt out
Reps9 balls (3 per hole)
GoalComplete 5/9 up-and-downs
Success: Save par more than half the time
Club Comparison
SetupSame spot, same swing, 3 different clubs
Reps3 balls with 7i, PW, LW each
GoalLearn your air/roll ratios
Note: Track where each ball lands and where it finishes to understand your personal ratios.

Quick Fixes

Having trouble? Tap to see solutions.

Cause: Ball too forward, weight on back foot, trying to scoop

Fix:

  1. Move ball to center of stance
  2. Put 70% weight on front foot and KEEP IT THERE
  3. Hands ahead of ball at address
  4. Think "brush the grass after the ball"

Cause: Standing up during swing, scooping, spine angle changes

Fix:

  1. Maintain spine angle throughout
  2. Keep head still - don't lift up
  3. Stay down through the shot
  4. Weight forward (60% front foot)

Cause: Wrong club, no landing spot, inconsistent swing length

Fix:

  1. Pick specific landing spot BEFORE every chip
  2. Match club to air/roll ratio needed
  3. Control distance with backswing length, not speed
  4. Same tempo for all chips - never decelerate
