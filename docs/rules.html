Quick Penalty Reference
Out of Bounds (white): +2, replay from original spot
Penalty Area (red): +1, drop within 2 club-lengths
Penalty Area (yellow): +1, drop behind hazard
Lost Ball: +2, replay from original spot
Unplayable: +1, 3 drop options
Remember: Drops are FREE - only swings count!

Detailed Rules

Out of Bounds (White Stakes)

Penalty: Stroke + Distance (+2 total)

  • Marker: White stakes or white lines
  • What to do: Return to where you played the last shot and hit again
  • Provisional Ball: If you think it might be OB, announce "provisional" and hit another ball before going to look
Example: Tee shot OB (1) + penalty (+2) = lying 3. Drop ball (free), hit it in hole (4). Total: 4
Local Rule E-5: Drop in fairway instead of going back. Still +2 but saves time! Check scorecard.

Penalty Areas (Red/Yellow Stakes)

Penalty: +1 stroke

Red Stakes (Lateral):

  • Drop within 2 club-lengths of where ball crossed, no nearer hole
  • Or drop on opposite side (same distance from hole)

Yellow Stakes (Regular):

  • Drop on line behind where ball crossed, going back as far as you want
  • Or replay from original spot (+1 only)

Both colors: You can always play it as it lies (no penalty) if possible!

Example: Tee shot in water (1) + penalty (+1) = lying 2. Drop (free), hit to green (3), putt in (4). Total: 4

Lost Ball

Penalty: Stroke + Distance (+2 total)

  • Search time: 3 minutes maximum
  • What to do: Return to where you played the last shot and hit again
  • Counting: Same as Out of Bounds
  • Provisional Ball: If you think it might be lost, announce "provisional" and hit another ball BEFORE going to look

If you find your ball within 3 mins, you must play it (unless you already played provisional from closer to hole)

Local Rule E-5: Same as OB - drop in fairway near where ball was lost instead of going back. Still +2. Check if course allows it.

Unplayable Lie

Penalty: +1 stroke

You can declare any ball unplayable anywhere (except in penalty area). You have 3 options:

  • Option 1: Drop within 2 club-lengths, no nearer hole
  • Option 2: Go back on a line from the hole through where ball was - as far back as you want
  • Option 3: Replay from where you hit the previous shot

In a bunker? Options 1 & 2 must stay in bunker (or +2 to drop outside)
