⛰️ Uneven Lies

Situations

Uphill Lie

  • Expect: Ball flies higher and left
  • Aim: Right of target
  • Club: More club (longer shot)
  • Setup: Shoulders parallel to slope, ball forward
  • Weight: On downhill foot for balance

Downhill Lie

  • Expect: Ball flies lower and right
  • Aim: Left of target
  • Club: Less club (shorter shot)
  • Setup: Shoulders parallel to slope, ball back
  • Challenge: Hardest shot in golf - take extra club for safety

Ball Above Feet

  • Expect: Ball tends to hook/go left
  • Aim: Right of target
  • Setup: Choke down on grip, stand more upright
  • Swing: Flatter swing plane

Ball Below Feet

  • Expect: Ball tends to fade/go right
  • Aim: Left of target
  • Setup: Flex knees more, bend from waist
  • Balance: Most difficult - prioritize solid contact
