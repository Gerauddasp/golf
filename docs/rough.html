Situations

Light Rough

  • Club Selection: Normal club or one less
  • Ball Position: Slightly back of normal
  • Swing: Steeper angle of attack
  • Tip: Expect reduced spin and more roll

Heavy Rough

  • Priority: Get back to fairway (don't be a hero!)
  • Club Selection: Hybrid or short iron for safety
  • If ball sitting up: Fairway wood possible with open face
  • Setup: Ball center, steep swing, firm grip
  • Accept: Punch out sideways if buried
