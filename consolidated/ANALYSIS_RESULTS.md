### Key Discovery - Attack Angle:
**PGA Tour Standard**: 0¬∞ (neutral) angle of attack
- NOT ascending like driver (+3-5¬∞)
- NOT descending like irons (-3-6¬∞)
- Perfect sweep motion required

### Primary Drill:
**Tee Clipping Drill** (AMG - appears in 3 videos)
- Place tee flush with ground
- Sweep without breaking = perfect
- Broken tee = too steep
- Missed tee = too shallow

### Tempo Finding:
"Don't rush the transition" - longer shaft needs more time
