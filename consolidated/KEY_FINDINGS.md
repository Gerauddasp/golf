### 1. **Driver Shaft Lean Must Be Nearly Vertical**
- **Finding**: 0-2¬∞ forward lean only (AMG)
- **Why It Matters**: Most amateurs have 8¬∞ (like irons) causing blocks/slices
- **Source**: Athletic Motion Golf biomechanics

### 2. **Stop-at-Top Width Fixes 90% of Slices**
- **Finding**: Maintaining arm width at top prevents over-the-top
- **Commitment**: 80% of drives for 2 months
- **Source**: Me and My Golf

### 3. **Fairway Woods Need Perfect Zero Attack Angle**
- **Finding**: PGA Tour average exactly 0¬∞ (not up, not down)
- **Drill**: Tee clipping - sweep without breaking
- **Source**: Athletic Motion Golf

### 4. **Connected Takeaway = Iron Consistency**
- **Finding**: Arms disconnecting causes 80% of poor strikes
- **Fix**: Towel under arms drill
- **Source**: Me and My Golf

### 5. **One Moving Part for Chipping**
- **Finding**: Chest rotation only, arms passive
- **Distance Control**: Turn size = shot distance
- **Source**: Andrew Cullen PGA

### 6. **Flop Shot Secret is Tempo, Not Face Angle**
- **Finding**: LONG and SLOW tempo matters most
- **Not**: Just opening the face
- **Source**: Athletic Motion Golf

### 7. **Eye Trigger Eliminates Putting Freezes**
- **Sequence**: Hole ‚Üí Ball ‚Üí GO (no pause)
- **Why**: Eliminates overthinking
- **Source**: Andrew Cullen PGA

### 8. **Weight Through Laces, Not Toes**
- **Finding**: Most amateurs on toes causing instability
- **Check**: Can you wiggle toes? Good!
- **Source**: Me and My Golf

### 9. **Lead Shoulder 5-6cm Forward for Iron Compression**
- **Finding**: Creates proper descending blow
- **Measurement**: 2-2.5 inches forward of setup
- **Source**: Athletic Motion Golf

### 10. **Hybrids Are True Blend (Not Woods or Irons)**
- **Attack Angle**: 0 to -2¬∞ (between both)
- **Setup**: Like iron, sweep like wood
- **Note**: Very limited instruction available

**Lower Rotational Finish** (Me & My Golf)
- Hands finish below shoulders
- Prevents flip through impact

### **FAT SHOTS**
**Set to Point Drill** (Me & My Golf)
- Short backswing
- Squeeze elbows through impact
- Short follow-through

### **THIN SHOTS**
**Eyes Ahead Drill** (Andrew Cullen)

## üîë SETUP KEYS BY CLUB

### **DRIVER**
- Ball: Inside lead heel (5-8cm)
- Weight: 55-60% back foot
- Spine: Tilted away from target
- Attack: +3-5¬∞ UP on ball
- Shaft: Nearly vertical at impact

### **FAIRWAY WOODS**
- Ball: Just inside lead heel (2-5cm)
- Weight: 50-50 balanced
- Attack: Exactly 0¬∞ (sweep)
- Feel: "Tickle the grass"

### **IRONS**
- Ball: Center to slightly forward
- Weight: Through laces (not toes!)
- Eyes: 3-4cm ahead of ball
- Attack: -3 to -6¬∞ (descending)
- Shaft: 20cm forward lean

### **WEDGES**
- Same as irons but choke down
- 5-yard increment system
- Less loft = more consistency

### **CHIPPING**
- SAME posture as full swing!
- Chip: Ball back, chest rotation
- Pitch: Ball center, toe-up backswing
- Flop: Long & slow tempo

### **PUTTING**
- Ball: Under left eye
- Face: 85% of direction
- Trigger: Hole‚ÜíBall‚ÜíGO
- Mentality: Always try to hole it
