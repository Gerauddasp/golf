# Chipping Drills - Summary

Five essential chipping drills develop short game consistency and confidence around the greens.
The consistent strike drill trains quiet hands with shoulder rotation instead of wristy movements.
The putt your chip drill builds confidence by practicing complete up-and-down sequences.
The chip within 3 feet drill develops distance control from varying distances and lies.
The bump and run drill teaches air-to-roll ratios for different clubs, while the hinge and hold drill masters Phil Mickelson's reliable chipping technique.
