# Putting Drills - Summary

Five essential putting drills develop distance control, stroke mechanics, aiming precision, and confidence on the greens.
The clockwise 1-2-3 drill builds distance control at three key lengths while managing break.
The straight back and through drill improves stroke path using alignment aids to reduce unwanted arc.
The center cup drill sharpens aiming precision by targeting a tee in the hole's center.
The lag putting drill prevents three-putts through pace control, while the don't leave it short drill builds aggressive confidence.
