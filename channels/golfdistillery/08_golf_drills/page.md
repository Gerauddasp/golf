# Golf Drills

Golf drills are exercises that you can use to focus on a specific area of your golf swing or for a specific golf shot.

Those practice routines can be performed at home, on practice ranges or practice areas, and they may involve the use of training aids.

## Drill Categories

### Backswing Drills
