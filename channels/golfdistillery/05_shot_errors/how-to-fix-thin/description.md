# How to Fix Thin Shots - Description

This page explains what thin shots are and why they occur when the club's leading edge hits below the ball's equator.
It identifies five major swing errors including standing up at impact, swaying, reverse pivot, flipping the club, and inconsistent spine angle.
Seven setup and address errors are detailed such as incorrect ball position, narrow stance, and improper weight distribution.
Each error includes a specific fix with actionable instructions to correct the problem and promote solid ball-first contact.
The comprehensive guide covers both in-swing mechanics and pre-swing setup adjustments to eliminate thin shots from your game.
