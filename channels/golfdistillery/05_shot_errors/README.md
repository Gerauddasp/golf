## Content Summary

The Shot Errors page catalogs 13 common golf shot problems organized into 4 categories:

### 1. Directional Errors (Lateral Ball Flight)
- Slice - Ball curves right
- Hook - Ball curves left  
- Push - Straight shot right of target
- Pull - Straight shot left of target

### 2. Contact Errors (Vertical Ball Flight)
- Fat - Club hits ground before ball
- Thin - Ball struck below equator
- Top - Ball struck above equator
- Sky - Ball struck by crown of club
- Whiff - Complete miss

### 3. Distance Errors
- Overclub - Ball goes past target
- Underclub - Ball falls short of target

### 4. Driver-Specific Flight Errors
- High Drive - Ball flies too high
- Low Drive - Ball flies too low
