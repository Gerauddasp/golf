## Content Summary

The Swing Tips page provides a comprehensive visual breakdown of the golf swing into 9 sequential phases:

1. **Setup & Address** - Foundation positioning
2. **Takeaway** - Initial club movement
3. **Backswing** - Continuation to the top
4. **Top of the Swing** - Transition point
5. **Downswing** - Movement toward impact
6. **Impact** - Ball contact position
7. **Release & Extension** - Post-impact arm extension
8. **Follow Through** - Completion phase
9. **Driver Technique** - Specific driver setup differences

Each phase includes:
- Dual-angle photographs (behind view and front view)
- Detailed written tips on body positioning
- Key checkpoints to monitor
- Common errors to avoid
