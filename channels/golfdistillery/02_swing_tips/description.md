# Description

Comprehensive visual guide to the golf swing broken down into 9 sequential phases from setup to follow through.
Each swing phase is illustrated with dual-angle photographs (behind and front views) showing proper positions.
Detailed written tips accompany each phase explaining body positioning, weight distribution, and club placement.
Special section dedicated to driver technique highlighting differences from iron shots in setup and swing plane.
Focus on fundamental mechanics and checkpoints golfers should monitor throughout their swing sequence.
