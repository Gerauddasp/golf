# Swing Drills - Description

This page serves as a comprehensive navigation hub for golf swing practice drills organized by swing phase.
The content presents 18 individual drills categorized across seven key stages of the golf swing from takeaway to follow through.
Each drill includes a descriptive image and brief explanation of its purpose for improving specific swing mechanics.
The drills progress sequentially through takeaway, backswing, top position, transition, impact, extension/rotation, and follow through phases.
This resource is designed to help golfers systematically practice and refine each component of their golf swing technique.
