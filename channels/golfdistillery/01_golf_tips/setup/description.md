Comprehensive guide to the four key elements of proper golf setup: alignment, stance and ball position, posture, and grip.
Emphasizes that golf is a "closed skill" requiring consistent, accurate setup to maximize swing development and shot consistency.
Covers eight detailed lessons including alignment (the #1 priority), stance width, distance to ball, ball position for each club, and hand position at address.
Provides practical drills and reference points, such as proper posture assessment and grip training techniques for home practice.
Stresses that repeating the same setup routine for every shot is essential for minimizing variation and improving ball striking accuracy.
