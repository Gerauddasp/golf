## Bunker Shot Types Covered

1. Regular Bunker Shot - Basic greenside bunker technique
2. Wet Sand (Compact Sand) - Hard surface adjustments
3. Plugged Lie - Ball buried in firm sand
4. Fried-Egg Lie - Ball in crater in fluffy sand
5. Upslope (Uphill) - Ball on bunker's green-side slope
6. Downslope (Downhill) - Ball on far-side slope
7. Long Bunker Shot - Maximum distance from flag
8. Short Bunker Shot - Minimal green to work with
9. Fairway Bunker - Distance bunkers away from green
