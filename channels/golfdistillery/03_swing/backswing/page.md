# Golf Backswing - How to Correctly Perform your Backswing (Golf Swing)

[Swing Tips for the Backswing][images/swing-tips-backswing.jpg]

The backswing follows the takeaway and extends until reaching the top of the swing. The page provides illustrated tips for proper execution.

## Key Backswing Tips

### 1. Left Arm Position

[Left Arm Straight][images/backswing_left-arm.png]

Should remain straight throughout

### 2. Left Heel

[Left Heel Grounded][images/backswing_left-heel.png]

Should stay grounded unless flexibility issues exist

### 3. Right Knee Flex

[Right Knee Flex][images/backswing-right-knee.png]

Maintain bend angle from address position

### 4. Left Knee Movement

[Left Knee Points to Ball][images/backswing_left-knee.png]

Should point toward ball or center of stance

### 5. Hip Rotation

[Hip Coil][images/backswing-all-arms.png]

Should coil gradually starting immediately

### 6. Hip Positioning

[Hips Stay in Box][images/backswing-hips-sway.png]

Stay in box; avoid sliding or swaying backward

### 7. Head Position

[Head Stable][images/backswing-head.png]

Remain stable; avoid vertical or horizontal movement

### 8. Weight Transfer

[Weight to Right][images/backswing-weight.png]

Move toward right foot during backswing

### 9. Backswing Rhythm

[Smooth Tempo][images/backswing-rhythm.png]

Proceed slower than downswing
