# Golf Impact - How to Correctly Position your Body and Club at Impact

[Swing Tips for the Impact][images/swing-tips-impact.jpg]

Impact is the only golf swing sequence where you actually make contact with the ball. Follow these tips in order to improve your impact position.

## Key Impact Position Checkpoints

### 1. Hands Position at Impact

[Hands Ahead of Ball][images/impact-hands.png]

Your hands should be ahead of the clubhead in what is referred to as 'keeping the lag'.

### 2. Knee Flex at Impact

[Maintain Knee Flex][images/impact-knees.png]

At impact your knees should retain some amount of flex still.

### 3. Hip and Hand Alignment

[Hips Rotated Toward Target][images/impact-release.png]

Your hips must have rotated far enough to be wanting to point towards the target.

### 4. Spine Angle Maintenance

[Maintain Spine Angle][images/impact-spine.png]

The spine angle that was set at address should be maintained on the downswing as well.

### 5. Head Position

[Eyes on Ball][images/impact-keep-your-head-down.png]

It is crucial that you find yourself actually looking at the ball as you hit it.

### 6. Club Selection Technique

[Ball-First Contact vs. Ascending Strike][images/impact-hitup-hitdown.png]

With irons, the club hits the ball first. With woods, strike the ball when the clubhead is rising.
