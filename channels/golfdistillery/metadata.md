# Golf Distillery Channel

## Channel Information
- **Website**: https://www.golfdistillery.com
- **Type**: Golf instruction website
- **Content**: Comprehensive golf tips, swing mechanics, drills, and terminology
- **Added**: 2025-10-30

## Overview
Golf Distillery is a comprehensive golf instruction website that provides detailed, illustrated guides covering all aspects of the game. The site is organized into clear categories focusing on different elements of golf technique, from basic setup through advanced shot-making.

## Content Categories

### Primary Instruction Sections
1. **Golf Tips** - General instruction for all skill levels
2. **Swing Tips** - Detailed swing sequence mechanics
3. **Swing Errors** - Common swing mistakes and corrections
4. **Shot Tips** - Specialty shots and difficult situations
5. **Shot Errors** - Ball flight problems and solutions
6. **Golf Tweaks** - Setup and equipment adjustments
7. **Swing Thoughts** - Mental cues and focus points
8. **Golf Drills** - Practice exercises and routines
9. **Golf Terms** - 200+ illustrated golf terminology definitions

## Key Features
- Fully illustrated guides with comparison images
- Video tutorials and demonstrations
- Progressive instruction from beginner to advanced
- Systematic organization by swing phase
- Comprehensive terminology dictionary
- Practice drills organized by skill area

## Content Organization Philosophy
The site follows a methodical approach to golf instruction:
- **Sequential Learning**: Content organized by swing sequence
- **Problem-Solution Format**: Errors paired with corrections
- **Visual Learning**: Heavy emphasis on illustrations and diagrams
- **Comprehensive Coverage**: From fundamentals to advanced techniques
- **Practical Application**: Drills and tweaks for immediate improvement
