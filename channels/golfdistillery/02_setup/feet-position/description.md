# Feet Position - Description

Comprehensive guide to feet positioning covering two main variables: stance width and foot flaring.
Explains four stance widths (wide, normal, narrow, very narrow) based on club and shot requirements.
Details four foot flaring options (no flare, left foot flared, right foot flared, both feet flared).
Wide stance provides maximum stability for driver; narrow stance for delicate shots near green.
Foot flaring affects rotational space in backswing and follow-through with various trade-offs.
