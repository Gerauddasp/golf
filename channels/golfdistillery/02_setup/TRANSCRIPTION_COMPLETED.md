### Stance Alignment (3 variations)

**Source URL**: https://www.golfdistillery.com/tweaks/setup/feet-position/stance-alignment/

1. **Open Stance** - `/02_setup/open-stance/`
   - Feet cross target line behind ball
   - Increases loft, creates outside-in path
   - Produces fade/slice ball flight
   - Images: 2 (stance diagram + effects)

2. **Square Stance** - `/02_setup/square-stance/`
   - Feet parallel to target line
   - Maintains designed loft
   - Produces straight ball flight
   - Images: 2 (stance diagram + effects)

3. **Closed Stance** - `/02_setup/closed-stance/`
   - Feet cross target line in front of ball
   - Decreases loft, creates inside-out path
   - Produces draw/hook ball flight
   - Images: 2 (stance diagram + effects)

### Ball Position (3 variations)

**Source URL**: https://www.golfdistillery.com/tweaks/setup/ball-position/ball-position-relative-to-feet/

4. **Ball Forward in Stance** - `/02_setup/ball-forward-in-stance/`
   - Ball closer to left foot
   - Increases loft, later contact
   - Higher trajectory, softer landing
   - Images: 2 (position diagram + effects)

5. **Ball in the Center of Stance** - `/02_setup/ball-in-the-center-of-stance/`
   - Ball equidistant from both feet
   - Neutral loft and contact point
   - Standard trajectory
   - Images: 1 (position diagram)

6. **Ball Back in Stance** - `/02_setup/ball-back-in-stance/`
   - Ball closer to right foot
   - Decreases loft, earlier contact
   - Lower trajectory, more roll
   - Images: 2 (position diagram + effects)

### Stance Width (4 variations)

**Source URL**: https://www.golfdistillery.com/tweaks/setup/feet-position/stance-width/

7. **Normal Stance** - `/02_setup/normal-stance/`
   - Feet shoulder-width apart
   - Balanced stability and mobility
   - Versatile for most shots
   - Images: 1 (stance diagram)

8. **Wide Stance** - `/02_setup/wide-stance/`
   - Feet wider than shoulders
   - Maximum stability, may restrict rotation
   - Best for driver and bunkers
   - Images: 1 (stance diagram)

9. **Narrow Stance** - `/02_setup/narrow-stance/`
   - Feet narrower than shoulders
   - Enhanced rotation, reduced stability
   - Ideal for wedges and pitch shots
   - Images: 1 (stance diagram)

10. **Very Narrow Stance** - `/02_setup/very-narrow-stance/`
    - Feet just inches apart
    - Maximum feel, minimal stability
    - Only for delicate chip shots
    - Images: 1 (stance diagram)
