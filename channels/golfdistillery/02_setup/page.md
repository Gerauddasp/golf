## Setup

The combination of all aspects related to the way the golfer's body is positioned relative to the ball, to the club in hand, and to the target before the beginning of a swing.

## Back Tilt

[Back tilt illustration][images/setup-back-tilt.png]

The golfer's back tilts forward toward the ball, enabling shoulder rotation and arm movement while holding the club.

## Knee Flex

[Knee flex illustration][images/setup-knee-flex.png]

Knees remain flexed rather than locked to maintain an active stance during the rotating motions of hips and shoulders.

## Arms and Hands Positions

[Arms and hands position illustration][images/setup-arms-hands-position.png]

Arms hang perpendicular to the ground from the shoulders, with hands typically positioned slightly ahead of the clubhead.

## Feet Position

[Feet position illustration][images/setup-feet-position.png]

## Grip

[Grip position illustration][images/setup-grip-position.png]

How the golfer holds the club, involving three main finger placements and three grip strength categories for non-putter clubs.

## Ball Position

[Ball position illustration][images/setup-ball-position.png]

Ball placement relative to feet varies by club type, shot type, and lie conditions.

## Additional Terms

### Aim

[Aim illustration][images/aim.png]

The direction a golfer wants his ball to go, towards the intended target.

### Alignment

[Alignment illustration][images/alignment.png]

Body positioning (feet, hips, shoulders) parallel to the target line for straight shots.

### Line

[Line illustration][images/line.png]

The path that the ball takes between the moment it is hit and the moment it comes to rest.

### Target Line

[Target line illustration][images/target-line.png]

The imaginary line that is formed between the ball and the target.
