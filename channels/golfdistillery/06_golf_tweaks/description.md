# Golf Tweaks - Description

This page serves as a comprehensive index to 20 illustrated guides covering adjustments golfers can make before taking shots.
The tweaks are organized into three categories: SETUP (16 articles covering ball position, feet position, clubface, grip, weight distribution, and club shaft), SWING (2 articles on driver accuracy and clubface follow-through), and CLUB (2 articles on shaft flex and gear effect).
Each article features detailed illustrations and explanations of how specific adjustments affect ball flight and shot outcomes.
The content emphasizes understanding the relationship between small setup or swing modifications and their impact on golf performance.
Topics range from fundamental concepts like grip strength and stance width to more technical subjects like gear effect and shaft lean dynamics.
