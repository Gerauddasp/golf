# Classic Golf Swing Channel

## About
This channel focuses on classic golf instruction, particularly based on Ben Hogan's legendary teachings from his book "Five Lessons: The Modern Fundamentals of Golf" - considered one of the most influential golf instruction books ever written.

## Content Focus
The "BEN HOGAN 5 LESSONS" series breaks down Hogan's fundamental approach to the golf swing:
1. The Grip
2. The Stance
3. The Backswing
4. The Downswing
5. Summary/Integration

## Teaching Philosophy
Ben Hogan's approach emphasizes:
- Precise mechanical fundamentals
- Building blocks approach (grip → stance → backswing → downswing)
- Repeatable, consistent swing mechanics
- Traditional techniques proven over time
