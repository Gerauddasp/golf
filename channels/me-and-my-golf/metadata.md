## About Me and My Golf

Me and My Golf is one of the most popular golf instruction channels on YouTube, featuring PGA professionals Piers Ward and Andy Proudman. Known for their clear, practical teaching style that breaks down complex swing mechanics into simple, actionable fixes.

## Teaching Approach

- **Practical Drills**: Easy-to-implement drills for range and home practice
- **Fault Correction**: Focus on fixing common swing faults (slice, early extension, over-the-top)
- **Pro Analysis**: Breaking down tour player swings and techniques
- **Equipment Insights**: Fitting sessions and equipment recommendations
- **Accessible Language**: Clear explanations without overly technical jargon

## Content Categories

- Driver technique and distance
- Iron ball striking and compression
- Short game (chipping, pitching, putting)
- Swing mechanics (backswing, downswing, impact)
- Common fault fixes
- Mental game and course management
- Equipment reviews and fittings

## Key Differentiators

- Dual instructor perspective (Piers & Andy)
- Mix of quick tips and comprehensive tutorials
- Collaboration with tour professionals
- TPI (Titleist Performance Institute) certified instruction
- Large library of troubleshooting content
