## ğŸ’¡ ANDREW'S CORE PRINCIPLES

These files embody Andrew's teaching philosophy:

1. **"Not everyone is the same"**
   - Find what works for YOUR body
   - Track YOUR progress

2. **"Men can typically only think of ONE thing at a time"**
   - Session planner forces you to pick ONE focus
   - Drill finder helps identify ONE key feel

   - Each drill includes "Key Feel" section
   - Progress tracker helps identify YOUR feels

   - No forced positions

   - Troubleshooting helps when you're stuck
   - But encourages self-discovery
