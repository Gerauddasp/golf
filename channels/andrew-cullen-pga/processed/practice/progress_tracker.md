## ‚úÖ SUCCESS METRICS BY STATION

**Use these to measure progress objectively**

### üöó DRIVER SUCCESS METRICS:

**BEGINNER Goals:**
- [ ] 7/10 balls start on target line or left (for slicers)
- [ ] Consistent contact (no tops/sky balls)
- [ ] Ball flight is predictable

**INTERMEDIATE Goals:**
- [ ] 7/10 fairways hit
- [ ] Consistent ball flight shape (draw or fade)
- [ ] Average distance tracking upward

**ADVANCED Goals:**
- [ ] 8/10 fairways hit
- [ ] Average 10+ yards gained from month ago
- [ ] Can shape ball on command (draw/fade)

**Current Level:** _______________
**Date Achieved:** _______________

---

### ‚öôÔ∏è IRON SUCCESS METRICS:

**BEGINNER Goals:**
- [ ] 7/10 ball-first contact
- [ ] Divot starts at or after ball
- [ ] No more thin/fat than 3/10

**INTERMEDIATE Goals:**
- [ ] 8/10 pure strikes
- [ ] Consistent divot pattern
- [ ] Can control trajectory (high/low)

**ADVANCED Goals:**
- [ ] 9/10 pure strikes
- [ ] Consistent distance gaps between clubs
- [ ] Strike ball from any lie (fairway, rough, divot)

**Current Level:** _______________
**Date Achieved:** _______________

---

### üî™ SHORT GAME SUCCESS METRICS:

**BEGINNER Goals:**
- [ ] Consistent contact (no chunks/skulls)
- [ ] General distance control (within 10 feet of target)
- [ ] Can get ball on green from fringe

**INTERMEDIATE Goals:**
- [ ] 7/10 chips within 6 feet of target
- [ ] Can control trajectory (low runner vs high lob)
- [ ] Confident from various lies

**ADVANCED Goals:**
- [ ] 8/10 chips within 3 feet of target
- [ ] Can stop ball quickly (spin control)
- [ ] Up & down 50%+ of time in practice

**Current Level:** _______________
**Date Achieved:** _______________

## üì± DIGITAL TRACKING TIPS

### Use Your Phone:

**Option 1: Notes App**
- Copy templates into new note each session
- Easy to search and reference
- Can add photos/videos inline

**Option 2: Spreadsheet App**
- More structured data tracking
- Can create graphs/charts
- Easy to see trends over time

**Option 3: Golf App**
- Many have practice tracking features
- Can link to on-course stats
- Automatic data visualization

**Option 4: Paper Journal**
- Physical log book
- Keep in golf bag
- Easy to reference at range
- Builds permanent record
