**[I] Drill 1D: Rib Stretch for Rotation** (8 min)
- **Fix:** Not creating space, no power, slice
- **What:** Stretch RIGHT rib away from ball on backswing

**[I] Drill 1E: No Lobster Grip** (8 min)
- **Fix:** Steep swing, throwing from outside
- **What:** Keep right hand SOFT (no squeezing index/thumb)
- **Key Feel:** "Soft right hand"
- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 1 - Intermediate - Drill 1E"

**[I] Drill 1F: Right Shoulder Behind** (5 min)
- **Fix:** Slice, outside-in path
- **What:** Keep right shoulder BEHIND in transition
- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 1 - Intermediate - Drill 1F"

### Power Generation

**[A] Drill 1G: Red Cross Drill** ‚ö†Ô∏è (10 min)
- **Fix:** Over-thinking, inconsistent path
- **What:** Draw red cross on ball, focus on hitting cross
- **Needs:** Sharpie to mark ball
- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 1 - Advanced - Drill 1G"

**[A] Drill 1H: Glide and Push** (10 min)
- **Fix:** No power, early extension
- **What:** Right pocket forward BEFORE top, then PUSH left foot
- **Key Feel:** "Glide and stomp"
- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 1 - Advanced - Drill 1H"

**[A] Drill 1I: Radius Maintenance** (5 min)
- **Fix:** Loss of power, disconnection

- **Key Feel:** "Hands to upper body"
- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 1 - Advanced - Drill 1I"

### Low Point Control

**[B] Drill 2A: Feet Together Drill** (8 min)

**[B] Drill 2B: Forward Tee Drill** ‚ö†Ô∏è (8 min)

**[B] Drill 2C: Eyes Ahead Practice** (4 min)
- **Fix:** Fat/thin, mental pressure
- **What:** Look at grass 3-4 cm ahead of ball (no props needed)

### Posture & Weight Transfer

**[I] Drill 2D: Tee to Right Pocket Drill** ‚ö†Ô∏è (10 min)

**[I] Drill 2E: Towel Drill** ‚ö†Ô∏è (8 min)

### Setup & Basics

**[B] Drill 4A: Thumbs Grip Option** (2 min)
- **Fix:** Poor grip feel, inconsistent roll
- **What:** Fit thumb joints together on putter grip
- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 4 - Beginner - Drill 4A"

**[B] Drill 4B: Ball Position & Eye Alignment Check** (3 min)
- **Fix:** Inconsistent start line, poor roll

**[B] Drill 4C: Confidence Builder with Tee** ‚ö†Ô∏è (6 min)
- **Fix:** Lack of confidence on short putts
- **What:** Putt to tee in ground, progressively longer distances
- **Needs:** Tee
- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 4 - Beginner - Drill 4C"

**[B] Drill 4D: Aim the Face** ‚ö†Ô∏è (5 min)
- **Fix:** Pushes/pulls
- **What:** Use alignment stick to check face aim
- **Needs:** Alignment stick or mat edge
- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 4 - Beginner - Drill 4D"

### Routine & Visualization

**[I] Drill 4E: Eye Trigger** (5 min)
- **Fix:** Freezing over ball
- **What:** Hole ‚Üí Ball ‚Üí GO (no delay)

- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 4 - Intermediate - Drill 4E"

**[I] Drill 4F: Visualization Practice** (5 min)
- **Fix:** Lack of commitment
- **What:** Visualize success 3 times (read + 2 looks during address)
- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 4 - Intermediate - Drill 4F"

**[I] Drill 4G: No Practice Swing** (5 min)

- **Reference:** practice_program.md "Station 4 - Intermediate - Drill 4H"

### Pressure & Special Shots

**[A] Drill 4H: Dennis Wise - Five Footers** ‚ö†Ô∏è (5 min)
