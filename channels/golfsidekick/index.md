### Key Progressions

**Tee Shot:**
- 100: Leave driver home (135m+ reliable club)
- 90: Most consistent long club (145m+)
- 80: Strategic driver use
- 70: Confidence-based selection

**Short Game:**
- 100: Bump-and-run only, two-putt
- 90: One chip club, lethal at 5 feet
- 80: 50%+ up-and-down, zero 3-putts
- 70: Elite scrambling, make everything inside 1.5m

**Mental Game:**
- 100: Don't count score
- 90: Zone entry, visualization
- 80: Positive self-talk
- 70: Think Box/Play Box system
